How to Use

The 10-20-30 Concept

Each 1-minute cycle repeats 5 times to form a 5-minute block:

  • 30 sec LOW — easy paddle, recover
  • 20 sec MODERATE — steady, comfortable pace
  • 10 sec HIGH — all-out sprint, max power

Program Details

Starting a Session

Tap START TODAY'S SESSION or any session card to launch the timer with the correct settings.

Timer Controls

  • PAUSE — pause / resume
  • SKIP — skip to the next phase
  • STOP — abort and return to schedule

Marking Sessions Complete

Sessions are auto-marked done when the timer finishes. Tap the circle on any card to manually toggle.

Steady-State Sessions (Advanced)

Shown with a dashed border. These are regular rowing at a steady pace for the indicated duration. Check them off manually when done.
