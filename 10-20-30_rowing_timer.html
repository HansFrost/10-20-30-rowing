How to Use

The 10-20-30 Concept

Each 1-minute cycle repeats 5 times to form a 5-minute block:

  • 30 sec LOW — easy paddle, recover
  • 20 sec MODERATE — steady, comfortable pace
  • 10 sec HIGH — all-out sprint, max power

Your Heart Rate Zones

Program Details

Training Days & Sessions Per Week

During setup you choose how many sessions per week (2–5) and which days to train on. Blocks per session ramp up gradually over the program—not every session in a week has the same count.

Moving a Session to a Different Day

Tap the ⇌ icon in the bottom-left corner of any session card. A picker appears where you choose a new day. The session keeps its original identity so your completion status is preserved. A yellow border and * mark indicate moved sessions. Tap Reset to default in the picker to undo.

Starting a Session

Tap START TODAY’S SESSION or tap any session card to launch the timer. Both interval and steady-state sessions have a guided timer with warm-up and cool-down.

Timer Controls

  • PAUSE — pause / resume
  • SKIP — skip to the next phase
  • STOP — abort and return to schedule

Marking Sessions Complete

Sessions are auto-marked done when the timer finishes. You can also tap the circle on any card to manually toggle completion.

Steady-State Sessions (Advanced)

Shown with a dashed border. Tap the card to start a countdown timer, or check them off manually. These build your aerobic base at a comfortable 65–80% max HR.

Block Progression

Blocks increase gradually over the program. Transition weeks mix higher and lower block counts within the same week, so the jump is never too sudden.

Free Session

Tap Free Session for an ad-hoc workout not tracked in the schedule. Choose blocks, rest time, warm-up, and cool-down.

Max Heart Rate

Set your max HR during setup or change it at the bottom of the schedule. All heart-rate zones update automatically.

Export & Import Progress

Scroll to the bottom of the schedule to Export your progress as a JSON file or Import a previous backup. Handy when switching devices.

Concept2 PM5

Set PM5 to “Just Row”. Prop your phone near the erg. ErgData can sync in the background.

Change Program / Reset

Tap Change Program to start fresh with a different program. Tap Reset Program at the bottom to clear all progress.
