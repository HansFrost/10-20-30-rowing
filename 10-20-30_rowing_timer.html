How to Use

The 10-20-30 Method

Each 1-minute cycle repeats 5 times to form a 5-minute block:

  • 30 sec LOW — easy paddle, recover
  • 20 sec MODERATE — steady, comfortable pace
  • 10 sec HIGH — all-out sprint, max power

Target Stroke Rates

  • LOW (30s) — 16–20 spm
  • MODERATE (20s) — 22–26 spm
  • SPRINT (10s) — 30+ spm
  • Warm-up / Cool-down — 18–20 spm
  • Steady-state — 20–24 spm

Stroke rate targets are shown on the timer during each phase. Use the PM5 stroke rate display to match.

Program Details

Schedule & Sessions

Training Days

During setup you choose how many sessions per week (2–5) and which days to train on. Blocks per session ramp up gradually over the program—not every session in a week has the same count.

Block Progression

Blocks increase gradually over the program. Transition weeks mix higher and lower block counts within the same week, so the jump is never too sudden.

Moving a Session

Tap the ⇌ icon on any session card to move it to a different day. The session keeps its identity so your completion status is preserved. A yellow border and * mark indicate moved sessions.

Marking Sessions Complete

Sessions are auto-marked done when the timer finishes. You can also tap the circle on any card to manually toggle completion.

Steady-State Sessions

Advanced program only. Shown with a dashed border. Tap the card to start a countdown timer, or check them off manually. These build your aerobic base at 65–80% max HR.

Training Times

Default Times

Tap Set Default Training Times at the bottom of the schedule to assign a default time for each training day (e.g. Tue 07:00, Thu 18:00). All sessions on that day will inherit the default.

Per-Session Times

Tap the time shown on any session card (or the set times banner for upcoming sessions) to override the default for that specific session.

Calendar Slots

When a time is set, the card shows a duration estimate (warm-up + blocks + rest + cool-down) so you can plan around your schedule.

Timer & Controls

Starting a Session

Tap START TODAY’S SESSION from the banner, or tap any session card to launch the timer. Both interval and steady-state sessions include warm-up and cool-down.

Quick Session

On training days, a Quick Session (1 block) button appears below the main start button. This runs just 1 block (~5 min with warm-up/cool-down) and still counts as a completed session—useful on busy days to keep your streak alive.

Controls

  • PAUSE — pause / resume
  • SKIP — skip to the next phase
  • STOP — abort and return to schedule

Free Session

Tap Free Session for an ad-hoc workout not tracked in the schedule. Choose blocks, rest time, warm-up, and cool-down.

Habit Tracking

Streak

Your streak counts consecutive completed sessions with no gaps. It appears in the habit strip below the progress bar. Your personal best streak is tracked automatically.

Stages

As you complete more sessions, you progress through three stages:

  • Getting Started (1+ sessions) — you’re trying something new
  • Building Momentum (4+ sessions) — you’re becoming a rower
  • Locked In (11+ sessions) — this is who you are

Milestones

You’ll earn milestone celebrations for achievements like your first session, streak records, completing a full week, and reaching program completion percentages.

Tip of the Day

A rotating daily tip appears on the schedule page with science-backed insights on the 10-20-30 method, rowing technique, and recovery.

Settings & Data

Rename Program

Tap the program name at the top of the schedule to give it a custom name. Press Enter or tap away to save.

Max Heart Rate

Set your max HR during setup or change it at the bottom of the schedule. All heart-rate zones update automatically.

Export & Import

Export saves your progress as a JSON file. Import restores a previous backup. Useful when switching devices.

Change Program / Reset

Tap Change Program to browse programs without losing your current progress. Tap Reset Program at the bottom to clear all progress.

Concept2 PM5

Set PM5 to “Just Row”. Prop your phone near the erg. ErgData can sync in the background.

Install as App

Tap below for instructions on installing this as a fullscreen app on your phone.

Install as App

For the best experience, install this as an app on your home screen. No app store needed — it runs fullscreen without browser bars.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button ⬆︎ at the bottom of Safari
  2. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”
  3. Tap “Add” in the top right

Android (Chrome / Brave)

  1. Tap the menu ⋮ in the top right
  2. Tap “Add to Home screen” or “Install app”
  3. Tap “Install” to confirm

The app stays in sync — updates appear automatically.
